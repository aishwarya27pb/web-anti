RIVO: The Telegram Bot That Dragged Me Into Tech Land

By Aishwarya P B | QA Automation Engineer & AI Enthusiast

Every great adventure starts with a bit of confusion. For me, it started with a "soft restart" and a whole lot of buffering.

The Day I Learned What a Bot Actually Is

There was a time when "bot" sounded like something lurking in sci-fi shadows. Then my friend casually announced, "Let's build a Telegram bot using Raspberry Pi," and my brain did a soft restart. I nodded, but internally I was buffering.

From Confusion to Curiosity

In the early days, everything felt like staring into fog. But the more we discussed, the more the picture sharpened. Soon we were on the hunt for the right AI tool to help us build our telebot, and after strolling through a forest of options, Google's Antigravity stood there like a digital guide waving a glowing stick.

We chose wisely.

Phase One: Birth of RIVO

Tiny Steps That Weren't Actually Tiny

We began with baby steps:

  • Adding a few commands
  • Setting up admin access
  • Teaching the bot to list tasks without whining

RIVO learned faster than expected. Then came the big dream — integrating a habit tracker. Because nothing says "self-improvement" like a bot nagging you about your tasks.

Antigravity, Our Silent Co-engineer

Every time we tossed a requirement at Antigravity, it returned something useful. Considerate, efficient, and never once asked for coffee.

Feature Fiesta

Leveling Up the Bot

The upgrades rolled in:

  • Multiple admin access
  • Replacing approval commands with clean little buttons
  • A /ip command that whispered device IPs like a friendly neighborhood spy

At this point, RIVO wasn't just a bot. It was a productivity sidekick.

The Plot Twist

The Day RIVO Went Silent

Everything was running smoothly... until RIVO decided to take an unapproved vacation. No responses. No errors. Just digital crickets.

We dove into debugging mode. Logs everywhere. Guessing games. Our AI assistant joined the investigation like a detective who charges per clue.

The Culprit: A Timeout

After enough troubleshooting to qualify us for a mini PhD, we found it — a timeout error masquerading as a grand crisis. Classic.

What's Next for RIVO

The Adventure Has Only Booted Up

Phase One may be complete, but the roadmap ahead is buzzing with features, integrations, and ideas that may or may not cause future plot twists.

But hey, that's the charm of building something from scratch.

Stay tuned — more chaos loading...
