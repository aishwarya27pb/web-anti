    About Me

    Journey in Quality Assurance

    I am a passionate Quality Assurance Specialist based in Bangalore, India. My journey in tech is driven by an obsession with precision and a commitment to digital excellence.

    Professional Experience

    Tata Consultancy Services (Oct 2022 - Present)

    As a QA Specialist at TCS, I focus on end-to-end functional and backend testing for large-scale enterprise applications. I have been instrumental in: - Developing robust automated scripts using Playwright and Selenium. - Implementing Page Object Model (POM) frameworks to enhance script maintainability. - Ensuring data integrity through deep SQL and Unix-based validation.

    Data Template InfoTech Pvt. Ltd (Jan 2020 - Oct 2022)

    In my previous role, I worked across various web and enterprise applications, mastering the art of: - Script design using Selenium, Robot Framework, and Cucumber (BDD). - API and Web Service validation using Postman. - Collaborating within Agile teams to iterate quickly without compromising quality.

    Personal Philosophy

    I believe that testing is not just about finding bugs; it's about understanding the user's journey and ensuring every interaction is seamless and reliable. My background in electronics and communication engineering (B.E.) provides me with a solid foundation for logical troubleshooting and system analysis.
